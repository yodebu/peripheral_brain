About Chris Albon

I am a data scientist, and the co-founder and Chief Science Officer at Popily. I am also the co-host of the data science podcast, Partially Derivative. Previously, I lead Ushahidi's work on crisis and humanitarian data and launched CrisisNET. Prior to Ushahidi, I was Director of the Governance Project at FrontlineSMS. I earned a Ph.D. in Political Science from the University of California, Davis researching the quantitative impact of civil wars on health care systems.

In 2008, I founded Conflict Health, a blog on the defense of health and health workers in armed conflict and political violence. I also wrote for the Daily Dot, United States Naval Institute Blog, TheAtlantic.com, ForeignPolicy.com, UN Dispatch, and elsewhere.

I earned a B.A. from the University of Miami, where I triple majored in political science, international studies, and religious studies.

I code in R and Python, and write in Markdown.
